    Liver Function (ALT/AST Enzymes)
    ALT and AST are enzymes located in liver cells that leak out into the general circulation when the liver cells are injured. They are good indicators of liver damage or injury from different types of diseases including: heart failure, liver tumors, viral hepatitis, and fatty liver. They also may indicate injury due to cholesterol-lowering and other medications.
