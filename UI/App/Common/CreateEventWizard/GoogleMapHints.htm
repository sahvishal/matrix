How to Input Latitude & Longitude for Incorrect Google Maps


Note Before Starting:
Often times Google Maps may be fixed by slightly modifying the hosts address.
For example: 8195 FM 78 Converse, Texas 78109 may display differently on Google maps than 8195 Farm to Market 78 Converse, Texas 78109. If you feel a simple correction of the host address will fix the Google map, please try this.
IMPORTANT : This is mandatory to varify the host address indicated by Google Map by clicking either “Yes” OR “No”.

Step 1: Click on Map within the BMS to pull up the Google Map

Step 2:
– Identify the location of the host address as indicated by the Google Map.
– If you find the location indicated by the Google Map is correct Please click “Yes” radio button.
– If you find the location indicated by the Google Map is incorrect Please click “No” radio button.

– Now you go to the google map and Place your mouse cursor directly on the location where the host should be and    “RIGHT CLICK”.
– Choose “What’s here?”

Step 3:
– This will place a green arrow on the correct location.
– Copy the new (correct) location information from the search bar in Google Maps.
– Paste the new (correct) location information into the BMS field “Corrected Latitude & Longitude”

Step 4:
View the new Google Map to verify that it is now correct.

Step 5:
Provide additional notes in the Technician Notes and Call Center Notes Section about where the location actually is. Refer to landmarks or any other information that helps clients and the tech team get to the location.
Example: This host site is on the NE corner of Fox Lane and Sara Road (also known as N2970 Rd). There is a CVS across the street. The screening will take place in the community room. Park in the back church parking lot and enter the green double doors. It will be the third room on your right.
