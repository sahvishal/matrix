ELECTROCARDIOGRAM (EKG)
The electrocardiogram monitors the electrical activity of the heart. Abnormal EKG waves can show a heart rate that is too fast, slow or irregular. It can disclose a past silent heart attack and can assess pacemaker function, among many other problems.
