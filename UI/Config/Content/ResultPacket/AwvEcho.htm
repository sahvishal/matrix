ECHOCARDIOGRAM ULTRASOUND
The echocardiogram is considered to be one of the best non-invasive methods of obtaining information about the heart’s size, strength, areas of weakness and valve function. Sound waves are used to create a video of the heart as it pumps blood throughout the body, allowing for a comprehensive evaluation of the heart’s function; only the coronary arteries are not directly seen.
All echocardiogram results must correlate with clinical findings. PCP follow up is required for further discussion of all Echo results and diagnosis.
