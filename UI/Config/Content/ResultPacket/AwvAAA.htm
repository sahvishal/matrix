ABDOMINAL AORTIC ANEURYSM (AAA)
The aorta is the body’s largest blood vessel, carrying oxygen-rich blood from the heart to the rest of the body. A healthy aorta has a diameter of less than 3cm and has walls that are strong and flexible. The abdominal aortic aneurysm ultrasound checks for enlarged areas of the aorta known as aneurysms. The danger from unknown aneurysms is that they can rupture without warning; this is a common cause of death among mature adults.
