All screening fees are due at the time of appointment scheduling. Gift certificates are valid for 1 year from the date of issue, are transferable, and can be used at any Matrix Medical Network location. Gift certificates are non-refundable and have no cash value. Cancellation fees do not apply for screening events sponsored by your employer.
